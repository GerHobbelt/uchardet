Dodge – koncernui FCA US LLC priklausanti markė, įkurta 1914 metais. Iki tol kol markę 1928 metais nupirko Chrysler Corporation, ji vadinosi Dodge Brothers Motor Vehicle Company.


== Logotipai ==
Star: 1914–1930,
Ram: 1932–1954
Crest: 1941–1957
Forward Look: 1955–1962
Fratzog: 1962–1976
Crest: 1976–1981
Pentastar: 1982–1992
Ram: 1993–2010 (ant pikapų nuo 1981 metų)
Dodge: 2010-
