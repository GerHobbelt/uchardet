Is meánscoil í Scoil Uí Chonaill (nó O'Connell School as Béarla) atá suite ar Shráid Richmond Thuaidh i dtuaisceart Bhaile Átha Cliath. Is í an mheánscoil Chaitliceach is sine i mBaile Átha Cliath agus í bunaithe ar dtús i 1827. 


== Stair ==
Ainmníodh an scoil as Dónall Ó Conaill mar gheall ar a iarrachtaí ar son shaoirse na gCaitliceach in Éirinn. Leag sé féin an bhunchloch agus bhronn sé 1,500 punt dá airgead féin ar an scoil. Bunaíodh í mar scoil do bhuachaillí ar dtús ach is scoil mheasctha í anois.
