Club peile Sasanach is ea Manchester City Football Club atá suite i Manchain. Imríonn an club sa Premier League agus sa staid, an City of Manchester Stadium.  
I mí an Mheithimh 2023,  fuair Manchester City an lámh in uachtar ar Manchester United i gcluiche ceannais an FA Cup. Cúpla lá níos déanaí, fuair City an “treble” nuair a bhain siad cluiche ceannais an Champions League.
