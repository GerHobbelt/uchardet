Is carachtar ficseanúil é Thomas "Tommy" Duckworth ar an sobalchlár teilifíse Briotanach de chuid ITV Coronation Street. Ghlac Darryl Edwards (1992–97), Joseph Aston (2000) agus Chris Fountain (2011–13) an pháirt mar Tommy.
