Baile in oirthear Chontae Mhaigh Eo is ea Coillte Mach (Kiltimagh as Béarla). Rugadh Antoine Raiftearaí an file sa mbaile seo. Is as an mbaile do Louis Walsh. Is féidir gur ó ainm an bhaile seo a thagann an focal béarlagair Béarla culchie nó "duine tuaithe".
