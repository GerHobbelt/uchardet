Is ceantar in oirthuaisceart Ghlaschú é Parkhead.
Tá staid Celtic Football Club, Celtic Park, lonnaithe ar Kerrydale Street, taobh siar ón London Road i bParkhead. Tugtar Parkhead ar an staid mar gheall ar an gceantar ina bhfuil an staid lonnaithe.
