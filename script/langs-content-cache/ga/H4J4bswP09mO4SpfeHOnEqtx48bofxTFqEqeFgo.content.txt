Is club sacair é Reading F.C. as Reading i Berkshire, Sasana, a bunaíodh sa bhliain 1871. Imríonn siad sa Madejski Stadium. Briseadh a mbainisteoir is deireanaí Brendan Rogers as a phost ar 16 Nollaig, 2009.
