Ameriyeh (Arabic: عامرية, romanized: Al-Amerīyyāh) or El Amiriye (Arabic: العامرية, romanized: Al-Amerīyyāh) is a village in northern Aleppo Governorate, northwestern Syria. Located some 8 km (5.0 mi) southeast of al-Rai and the border with Turkey, it is administratively part of Nahiya al-Rai in al-Bab District. In the 2004 census, Ameriyeh had a population of 881. The village is inhabited by Turkmen.
