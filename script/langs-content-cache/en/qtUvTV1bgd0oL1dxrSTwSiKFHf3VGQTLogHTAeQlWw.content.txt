Olympia is an unincorporated community in Bath County, Kentucky, United States. The community is located along Kentucky Route 36 5 miles (8.0 km) southeast of Owingsville. Olympia has a post office with ZIP code 40358.Olympia is near the site of the 1876 Kentucky meat shower.
