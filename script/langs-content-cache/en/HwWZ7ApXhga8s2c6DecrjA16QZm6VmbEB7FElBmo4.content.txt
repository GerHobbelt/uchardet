Volim biti zaljubljena (English: I love being in love) is the third album by Croatian singer Lana Jurčević. It was released in 2008, by Menart Records. The main song, "Volim biti zaljubljena", was featured on the Croatian TV show Nad lipom 35, along with "Pronađi me".
