Stéphane Cassard (born 11 November 1972) is a French former professional footballer who played as a goalkeeper for Sochaux, Le Havre, Montpellier, Créteil, Troyes, RC Strasbourg Alsace and US Boulogne.He later worked as a goalkeeping coach at Marseille.
