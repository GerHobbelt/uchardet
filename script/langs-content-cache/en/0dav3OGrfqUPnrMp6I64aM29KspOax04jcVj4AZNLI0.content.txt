Hajji Malek Shirzayi (Persian: حاجي ملك شيرزايي, also Romanized as Ḩājjī Malek Shīrzāyī) is a village in Margan Rural District, in the Central District of Hirmand County, Sistan and Baluchestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 95, in 17 families.
