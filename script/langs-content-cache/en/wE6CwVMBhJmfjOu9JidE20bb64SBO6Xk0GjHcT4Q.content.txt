Sulgostów [sulˈɡɔstuf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Klwów, within Przysucha County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. It lies approximately 2 kilometres (1 mi) south of Klwów, 17 km (11 mi) north of Przysucha, and 82 km (51 mi) south of Warsaw.
