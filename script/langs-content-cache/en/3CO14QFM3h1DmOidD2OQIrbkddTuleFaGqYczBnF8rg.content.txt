Bab Mishan-e Bala (Persian: باب ميشان بالا, also Romanized as Bāb Mīshān-e Bālā) is a village in Sarduiyeh Rural District, Sarduiyeh District, Jiroft County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 146, in 33 families.
