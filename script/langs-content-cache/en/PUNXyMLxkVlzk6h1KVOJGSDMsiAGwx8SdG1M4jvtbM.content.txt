John T. Brown (March 14, 1876 â€“ January 18, 1951) was an American politician who served as the 42nd lieutenant governor of Ohio from 1929 to 1931 serving under Governor Myers Y. Cooper.


== Biography ==
Brown was born in Plain City, Madison County, Ohio.  He was a Republican and a Methodist.
He died January 18, 1951, in Madison County, Ohio, of congestive heart failure.
