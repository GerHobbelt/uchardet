123 km (Russian: 123 км) is a rural locality (a passing loop) in Osinogrivskoye Rural Settlement of Topkinsky District, Russia. The population was 29 as of 2010.


== Geography ==
The passing loop is located on the Yurga-Tashtagol line, 30 km south of Topki (the district's administrative centre) by road. Znamensky is the nearest rural locality.
