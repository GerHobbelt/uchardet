Ahmad Akbari Javid (Persian: احمد اکبری جاوید; 10 February 1947 – 17 October 2022) was an Iranian fencer. He competed in the individual and team foil and team sabre events at the 1976 Summer Olympics.Akbari died on 17 October 2022, at the age of 75.
